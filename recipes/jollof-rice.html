Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a type of nigerian rice, which is orange.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Onions
  • Sctoch Bonnet
  • Bell peppers aka tashse
  • Salt
  • Bay Leaf
  • Vegtable Oil

Steps

  1. Place the base ingredeints[bell peppers, onions, sctoch bonnet, tomatoes] in a blender and blitz until smooth.
  2. Put vegtable oil in a pot with a medium heat. Add onions, add tomatoe paste.Fry while string.Keep string for three-five mintues.
  3. Pour in the blended base, combine and bring to a simmer.Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover with a lid.Cook but stir occsionly until the sauce is reudced and the oil begins to separte from the sauce, 12-15 mins.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, thyme, stock cubes, bay leaves and water.Season generously with salt and pepper, to taste, then cover and bring to a boil over a medium-high heat.
  5. Meanwhile, rinse the rice throughly with cold water until the water runs clean then drain.Add the rice to the sauce and stir to combine.As soon as it comes to boil reduce to heat to low cover the pot and cook for 25 mins.
  6. Remove the bay leaves and stir then it is finnaly ready.
