Fried Plantian

Desription

This a side dish which is a something that goes with a main dish.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Vegtable oil
  • Plantian[the fruit]

Steps

  1. Using a sharp knife, cut the plantian into the shape/size of your choice.
  2. Place the plantians in a large bowl, sprinkle with salt and put it a side.
  3. Put on the cooker.Put frying pan in the cooker.Put the oil and let it get a bit hot.Put your plantian in and leave there until it's golden brown.Then remove from oil and put on a tissue paper to take away the oil.
