Noodles

Description

Noodles with food like different meat, eggs etc;

This is a savoury dish which many people enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Starch
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. You combine to make a dough then rest for half an hour.
  2. Flatten the dough into a thin sheet with a rolling pin.
  3. Fold the sheet then cut into narrow strips.Unfold and loosen the noodles.
  4. Cook the noodles in boiling water.
